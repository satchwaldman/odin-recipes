Fruit Smoothie

Home

Description

This yogurt smoothie recipe is delicious! You may substitute the strawberries for any other berries or fruit. Served immediately after making.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Directions

  1. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Serve right away after making and enjoy!
